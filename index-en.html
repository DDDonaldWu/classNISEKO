About Us

CLASS NISEKO is a private ski lesson team based in Niseko, Hokkaido. We offer English and Chinese support, and our lessons are ideal for first-time skiers, families, and those looking to improve their skills.

Lesson Locations

Our private lessons take place mainly around the Niseko area. We select the most suitable resort and meeting point based on your needs.

  • Annupuri — Wide and gentle slopes, beginner-friendly
  • Hirafu — The largest resort in Niseko, full facilities
  • Hanazono — Varied terrain, ideal for intermediate training
  • Private Area — Exclusive private land, safe and stress-free for beginners

The meeting point will be confirmed after booking. If you're unsure which resort to choose, we’re happy to advise.
